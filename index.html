My work

Caerleen

Caerleen is a fictional univere that I've been working on for two years. Inspired by the Witcher saga, by Andrzej Sapkowski, I had the idea of writing the evolution of a whole continent.
Today, more than fifty character are existing in the world of Caerleen, distributed on a two thousand years history. Even if the stories are not fully written yet, I draw my characters on a daily basis.

Betrayal

Betrayal is another fiction that I am creating along with Caerleen. Much shorter than the previous story, in a sci-fi environnement, I hope to be able to finish writing it and to publish it as a web comic.

Memento Mori

Memento is the final project I had to do for my professional degree in Game design, for which I was the lead artist and the character artist.
In this game, you are playing as a mother morning the death of her daughter, and the said daughter, in a journey of motherly love and acceptance.

Living Rock

Living Rock is a game develloped in 2 days during a game jam. The game is telling the story of a young warrior, magically linked to his old instructor, who has been turn into a crystal.
During this two day experience, I have been one of the two artists in charge of the visual of the game.

Putch

Putch is a board game that I worked on during a week of game jam. During the game, you are playing as a politician, embarked in the election against the dictator of the country. To avoid being the last one facing him on the last round of the election, you will have to do everything in order to damage your reputation, and help your opponent to win the favors of the people.
Some of the card illustrations have been drawn by Sally Niasse.

Fragment of Future

Fragments of Future was the last project due to complete my two years degree about Multimedia and Internet Technologies. As the only graphist in a group of five students, I was in charge of the visual of the game.
Fell free to check out Fragments of Future.
